Design FX: How Nebulas Inspired the Look of Spider-Man’s Latest Archenemy 

In The Amazing Spider-Man 2, Jamie Foxx stars as Electro, the sinister glowing antagonist out to get Andrew Garfield’s Spidey. In this video, FXguide’s Mike Seymour explains how Sony Pictures Imageworks used a combination of makeup and CGI to turn the Academy Award winner into the blue, electrically-charged villain—using inspiration from lightning, neurological networks, and even deep-space nebulas.
